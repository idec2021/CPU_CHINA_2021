MUTANT CONSTRUCTION

PCR REACTION SYSTEM

  • Configure the following PCR system:
1. Template 1 ul
2. Polymerase and buffer 10 ul
3. F-primer 1 ul
4. R-primer 1 ul
5. ddH2O 7 ul
  • Perform the following PCR program:
1 .Thermal denaturation 98℃ 3 min
2. denaturation 98℃ 1 min
3. Annealing 54℃ 30 sec
4. Elongation 72℃ 4 min
30-35 cycles from step 2 to start the cycle
5. Complementary extension 72℃ 10 min

HOMOLOGOUS RECOMBINATION SYSTEM CONSTRUCTION

  1. Perform agarose gel electrophoresis on PCR products
  2. Cut out the gel block containing the band of interest and use FastPure Gel DNA Extraction Mini Kit (Vazyme Biotech) to recover the DNA.
  3. Digest the recovered product with Dpn Ⅰ enzyme, set up the following system：
    10 × Buffer Tango (with BSA) 2 ul
    Dpn Ⅰ(10U/ul) 1 ul
    DNA 17 ul
    total 20 ul
    The mixture is incutated in water bath at 37℃ for 1~5 hours.
  1. Set up the following homologous recombination system:
    DNA 20 ul
    5 × CE Ⅱ Buffer 6 ul
    Exnase Ⅱ 4 ul
    The mixture is incutate in water bath at 37℃ for 30 min and immediately place the tube at 4°C or on ice.

TRANSFORMATION OF ESCHERICHIA COLI

  1. Place the competent cells (DH5α) on ice
  2. Pipet 10 μl of the recombination products to 50 μl of the competent cells, flip the tube several times to mix thoroughly, and then place the tube on ice for 30 min.
  3. Heat-shock at 42°C for 45 sec and then immediately chill on ice for 2 - 3 min.
  4. Add 450 μl of LB medium (without antibiotics) to the tube. Then, incubating at 37°C, 220 rpm for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat the LB plate which contains appropriate selection antibiotics at 37°C.
  6. Centrifuge the culture at 4, 500 rpm for 3 min, discard 400 μl of supernatant. Then, re-suspend the pellet with 100 μl of remaining medium and plate it on an agar plate which contains appropriate selection antibiotic.
  7. Incubate at 37°C for 12 -16 hours.
  8. Selected a single colony and added it to 250 ml LB medium (with 1‰ Amp), inculated at 37℃, 180 rpm.
  9. Used EndoFree Maxi Plasmid Kit (TIANGEN) to extract plasmids.

YEAST TRANSFORMATION

PROCEDURES FOR LINEARIZATION OF THE VECTOR

  1. Set up the following enzyme digestion system for vector linearization:
    plasmid 10 ug
    FD Sac Ⅰ 5 ul
    FD Buffer 5 ul
    ddH2O to 50 ul
    The mixture was incubated at 37℃ for 1 h.

Electrotransform

  1. Used Thermofisher GeneJET PCR Purification Kit to desalt the linearized mixture.
  2. Electrotransform the linearized vector into Pichia pastoris GS115 competent cells.[Voltage: 1.5 kV; capacitance: 25 uF; resistance: 20 Ω. Shock time: 4-10 msec]
  3. Apply 200-600 μl bacterial body fluid to MD plate.
  4. The plate was incubated at 28℃ until single colony appeared.

INDUCED EXPRESSION

PREPARATION OF CULTURE MEDIUM (BMGY & BMMY)

FERMENTATION

  1. Select a single yeast colony and incubated in a 250 ml flask with 50ml BMGY medium and cultured at 28 ℃, 280 rpm.
  2. When the yeast grew to OD600= 2-6, centrifuge at room temperature at 4000 rpm for 5 min, the thallus was collected and resuspended with BMMY to adjust OD600=1.0, transfered to a 2L flask.
  3. The yeast was incubated at 28 ℃, 280 rpm. Add chlorhematin to a final concentration of 0.1 g/L.
  4. Add methanol to the medium every 24 h, until the concentration was 2%.

COLLECTION AND PURIFICATION OF PROTEIN

SEPARATION OF THE THALLUS AND PROTEIN

  1. The yeast were initially collected by centrifuged at 4000 rpm for 20 min. This step produces thallus and supernatant.
  2. And the supernatant was centrifuged at 15000 rpm for 30 min twice.
  3. The final supernatant was collected as the crude enzyme solution.

PROTEIN PURIFICATIION

  1. Add a certain amount of ammonium sulfate solid (to about 60% saturability) to the crude enzyme solution for salting out.
  2. 15000 rpm, 20 min, discard the supernatant.
  3. Precipitate is dissolved in an equal volume of protein buffer (10% glycerol, 500 mM NaCl and 20 mM HEPES.) and stored to -80℃.

ENZYME ACTIVITY ASSAY

  1. Add 20 μl enzyme solution to 140 μl solution containing 0.4 mM MnSO4, 50 mM sodium malonate and 1 mM 2, 6-DMP.
  2. Add 40 ul 0.1 mM H2O2 to initiate the reaction.
  3. Using microplate reader to immediately detect the absorbance of the solution at 469 nm.
