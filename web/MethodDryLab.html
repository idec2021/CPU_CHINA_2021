PLMC

PLMC infers undirected graphical models to describe co-evolution and co-variation in biological sequence families. With a multi-sequence alignment as an input, PLMC can quantify inferred coupling strengths between all pairs of positions (couplingsfile output) or infer a generative model of the sequences for predicting mutation effects or designing new sequences (paramfile output).
