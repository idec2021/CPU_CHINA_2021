Manganese Peroxidase Directed evolution

Directed Evolution of Manganese Peroxidase Based on Semi-rational Design.

Examplified by polyethylene, undegradable plastics have caused severe environmental crisis worldwide.

In order to deal with the huge number of plastic waste, we introduced MnP as the critical enzyme of our molecular machine.

However, as our molecular machines are put into use, MnP will have to face the complex environments in the real world.......

In order to enable MnP to adapt to the complex working environment to the greatest extent, we have carried out directed evolution on it, aiming to improve the pH/temperature/organic solvent stability of MnP.
